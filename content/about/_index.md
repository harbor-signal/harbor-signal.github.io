---
title: "About Ingrid Watch"
description: "A public home for Ingrid's harbor observations and science-fiction criticism."
---

Ingrid Watch is a two-part notebook: a harbor traffic observatory and a science-fiction review space. The point is not maritime decoration or fandom recap. The point is movement, maintenance, authority, scarcity, bodies, and systems that either hold or fail.

The harbor side begins with authored observations and source notes. The review side uses a consistent grading frame: logistics, governance, bodies, longing, and verdict.
